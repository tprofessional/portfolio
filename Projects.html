Project: Scratch Platformer- Invasion of the Triangles

Course CSP (Computer Science Principles)

Duration: Approximately 2 weeks (August 2018 - September 2018)

Objective: Use Scratch to create a short game and/or interactive story.

Type of Project: Pair Project


This project was my first coded game in Scratch. During the two weeks my partner and I were given to develop our project, we brainstormed ideas while trying to decide what type of interactive program we wanted to create, wrote instructions, received feedback, organized our game, reflected on our final product, and logged our progress. Our documentation was created with the intent to look back at it and compare our work with any future ideas we had that required a similar developmental process. This project helped me develop an understanding of the fundamentals of using code to build a game, or any other type of program. Prior to beginning of the project, we were introduced to different types of variables and how they were used in professional coding to do different tasks. Difficulties in developing our program usually revolved around the limited types of blocks we were able to utilize, as we were sometimes puzzled about how to use the blocks to carry out a certain portion of our ideated function. In this project, me and my partner’s roles were split into Main Programmer and Main Notebooker. We agreed beforehand that I’d be in charge of the documentation. Overall, my partner and I worked well together; we shared our ideas, improved upon and fixed bugs in the code, and both contributed to the documentation of the project.
